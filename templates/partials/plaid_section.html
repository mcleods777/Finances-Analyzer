Bank sync (Plaid)

Automatically pull transactions and balances from linked banks

Plaid isn't configured yet. To enable live bank sync:

  1. Sign up for a free Plaid account at dashboard.plaid.com/signup — the free Trial plan ("Personal use") covers about 10 institutions.
  2. Copy .env.example to .env in the project root.
  3. Fill in PLAID_CLIENT_ID and PLAID_SECRET from your Plaid dashboard (keep PLAID_ENV=sandbox to test, switch to production for real banks).
  4. Restart the app.

Until then, CSV upload keeps everything working.
